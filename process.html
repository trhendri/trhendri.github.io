This is the final draft of the index.
"Less is more" and "keep it simple".

This was the 3rd draft of the index of this site. The idea was a minimalist single page layout. I attempted to layout information in a simple way. I also added a gradiant to the header and nav. It has since been recycled and edited on this site as a sort of "map" for easy reference.

This is the technically 2nd draft of this site's index. I wanted to use the color coral, or a color similar to it. No real styling attempted too much on this. It just had a nav bar and header with simple links. I decided to keep this drafts nav and header for the internal pages.
